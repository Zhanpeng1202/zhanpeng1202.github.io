Zhanpeng Luo

I'm a senior undergraduate student majoring computer science at the University of Pittsburgh. I will join the Johns Hopkins University as a PhD student in 26 Fall. I feel fortunate to be supervised by Jaemin Cho.

Previously I work with Katia Sycara and Yaqi Xie. I work on reconstruction, view synthesis, and world modeling.

I am now interested in data-driven method for 3D perception and generation, and I bet on video world model to solve it.

Things I would like to research on the next stage:

  • Generative 3D reconstructor that can hallucinate the unseen regions and generalize from object-level to scene-level.
  • Post-training for world model for better 3D consistency, camera controlbility and generalibility.
